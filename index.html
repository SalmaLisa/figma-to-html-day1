Introduces Green Coal

We are introducing Green Coal from Municipal Solid Waste by Macawber Beekay. An eco-friendly and carbon-neutral waste conversion process with a daily capacity of 600T!

The Challenge

High Quantity Of Co2 Production & Water Contamination Due To Fossil Coal.

The goal of Green Coal technology is to lessen the adverse effects on the environment by utilizing a variety of cleaning and emission-controlling technologies.

Excessive Mining Leading To Deforestation

Water Contamination Due To Extraction Process

Large Amount Of Toxic Emission & Pollutants

The Impact

An Impact Which Facilitates Sustainability & Stability.

Bio-coal is carbon-neutral as it is derived from Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) without any mining of fossil fuels. Using 1 kg of bio-coal as solid fuel, by replacing fossil coal brings to a reduction of approx. 2 kg CO2 produced per kg of fossil coal. Bio-coal can offer a sustainable and fossil-free alternative for industries such as metallurgy
